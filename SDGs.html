Project on SDGs

What is SDGs?

In 2015, world leaders committed to achieving 17 Sustainable Development Goals, which aim to end poverty, reduce inequality, address climate change, etc. by 2030.

SDGs Goal No.17 Partnership for the Goals



Within the SDGs goal, I pick up the 17th goal: Partnerships for the goals.

I believe that this goal holds importance because Global goals can only be achieved if we work together. Developing countries need international investment, fair trade, and market access to support the development of innovative technologies.
Building a better world requires us to be collaborative, empathetic, inventive, passionate, and above all, collaborative.

Q. What can we do to help achieve this goal?


These are some things we can do in daily life:
  1. Support exports by buying products from developing countries.
  2. Pay attention to the Fair Trade label on products which helps to indicate products from local producers in developing countries.
